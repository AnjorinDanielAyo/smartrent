Features
Everything you need in one place

Verified listings

Every item listed goes through a review process to ensure quality and accuracy for renters.

Real-time availability

See exactly when items are available and book instantly with no double-booking conflicts.

Dual roles

Be a renter, an owner, or both. Your account supports both modes with separate dashboards.

Earn passively

List items you're not using and earn rental income — track your earnings in real time.

How it works
Simple from start to finish
1

Create your account

Sign up as a renter, owner, or both. Set up your profile in under a minute.

2

Browse or list

Renters browse by category or search. Owners add photos and set their daily rate.

3

Request & approve

Send a rental request. Owners review and approve it. You both get notified instantly.

4

Rent & return

Pick up or arrange delivery. Return by the agreed date and leave a review.
